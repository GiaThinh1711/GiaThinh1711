About me

Hi everyone!

Welcome to my little corner! I'm a Vietnamese, currently 24 years old, full of enthusiasm, and a student majoring in Interactive Design and Technology at Saskatchewan Polytechnic, Regina. Life in Canada isn't just about classes; it's also a journey of pushing my limits, from a young designer to a family man, always striving for a better future for myself and my beloved wife back in Vietnam.

To keep my mind constantly inspired, I choose to live in harmony with music and nature. Having played the piano for five years, I enjoy the feeling of letting my soul wander with the sound of the instrument or humming a few tunes after a stressful day at work. Music has honed my attention to detail and rhythm in design.

In addition, I am a person who especially loves dogs and cats and has a passion for aquarium fish. Taking care of these little friends and watching the peaceful world in the fish tank helps me learn patience and how to observe the smallest details.
